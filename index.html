Who We Are

Practice-first consulting for agencies that serve people.

Savin Hill Consulting was founded on a simple principle: technology and process should serve practice — not the other way around. We work with health and human services professionals at the national, state, and local levels to improve the way agencies deliver services, lead teams, and manage change.

We don't hand off reports and walk away. We get in the work with your team — assessing what's real, building what's needed, and staying until it sticks.

Practice Over Technology

We understand frontline work and design solutions around how people actually do their jobs — so they spend time with people, not struggling with systems.

Collaboration Is Non-Negotiable

Working together isn't a nice-to-have. It's the critical skill that makes programs grow and outcomes improve. We build it into everything we do.

Sustainable Solutions

We build internal capacity — not dependency. Our goal is to leave your agency stronger, more capable, and more resilient than we found it.

People at the Center

What We Do

Deep HHS expertise, applied where it matters.

Our consulting practice spans the full breadth of health and human services — from frontline practice improvement to enterprise-level strategic planning.

Practice & Policy Consulting

Needs assessments, practice improvement strategies, and policy analysis for state and local HHS agencies. Translating complex regulations into practical, frontline-ready guidance.

Change Management

CCMP-certified change management for organizations navigating system transitions, technology rollouts, and restructuring. We make change stick — not just happen.

Child Welfare & Family Services

CCWIS feasibility studies, CFSR preparation, continuous quality improvement, and Systems of Care implementation. Deep expertise across every level of child welfare practice.

Strategic Planning

Organizational strategic plans, Human Services Value Curve implementation, and infrastructure development for HHS departments ready to move from reactive to proactive.

Continuous Improvement

Lean Six Sigma-informed process improvement for HHS agencies. Streamlining business processes so teams focus on serving people, not fighting paperwork.

Leadership & Capacity Building

Developing the skills of HHS leaders to navigate complexity, manage through change, and build high-performing teams — grounded in real-world experience, not theory.

Our Founder

Andrea Richardson, CCMP, LSSGB

Andrea founded Savin Hill Consulting after more than 30 years working in health and human services — not from the sidelines, but in the work itself. From front-line child welfare practice to managing multi-million dollar federal initiatives, she built a career around one belief: put practice first, and the right solutions follow.
